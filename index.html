Why Sponsor Us?
  1. Exposure at conference
  2. Access to Students
  3. Build the Community

Over the past three years the Computer Science Department at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute has had a strong initiative to support females in Computer Science. A large part of this initiative is to send women in conferences to get more exposure to the variety of topics within the field.
